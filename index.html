Hiranyagarbha

is an initiative based on the preventive concepts started to reduce Maternal Mortality Rate, Infant Mortality Rate & Malnutrition and ensure proper Mental and Psychological development

  • Reducing the MMR & IMR significantly

  • Spreading medical literacy

  • Attainment of highest intellectual & physical growth of newborn

PROCESS FOLLOWED

Six different Stages are there that marks the working our initiative

1

Information collection & Categorization of High-risk mothers by field workers by door-to-door survey

Learn more

2

Collected information disseminated from ANM to BMOs & from Anganwadi workers to PO, ICDS.

Learn more

3

Then joint team of PO, ICDS & BMO will approach the mother & their family to resolve health issues. Medical & Nutritional Advice given to the mother.

Learn more

4

7 days later, Re check-up of the mother to check whether the mother is recovering properly or not?

Learn more

5

This process will continue till the complete recovery of the mother otherwise the mother will be admitted to the hospital.

Learn more

6

The information collected, reviewed by me & also talk with the mothers to get the feedback

Learn more

  • The IMR has reduced by 48%
